About Us

InAmigos Foundation is a registered non-profit organization dedicated to creating positive social impact across India. Founded in 2020, the NGO focuses on education, community welfare, women empowerment, environmental sustainability, animal welfare, and youth skill development.

Our Ongoing Projects

```

Project BachpanShala

Supporting underprivileged children through education, digital literacy and learning opportunities.

Project SEVA

Providing food, clothing and essential support to underprivileged communities.

Project JEEV

Feeding and caring for stray animals while promoting animal welfare awareness.

Project UDAAN

Empowering women through skill development, awareness programs and financial independence initiatives.

Project PRAKRITI

Promoting environmental sustainability through plantation drives and eco-friendly practices.

Project VIKAS

Providing internships and skill-building opportunities for students and young professionals.

```

Social Impact

  • 50,000+ meals and clothing support distributed.
  • 20,000+ saplings planted for environmental conservation.
  • Thousands of youth trained through internships and skill programs.
  • Women empowerment and community development initiatives across India.
  • Daily support for stray animals through volunteer networks.
